Internship Project

Internship Background
Company : Telekom Malaysia Berhad (TM)
Division : Network Operation Northern
Department : External Network Zone
Project Background

Working at TM Node Sebrang Jaya mainly involves interaction with contractors. The contractors handle problems such as stolen cables, tilting or toppling of electricity poles and slacking of wires. These problems are fixed when there are complaints received through the official TM customer service platform. However, over the years customers have been giving complaints and reports directly to the contractors through unofficial platform, mainly WhatsApp. This results in the monthly report of work done by the contractors is lower than the actual effort as they are not officially recorded in the official system. Hence, this project intends to allow the contractors to store information of any extra work performed providing proof of effort for monthly report to the higher ups.
